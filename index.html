Fall 2024 Experiments



Students will experience three augmented reality-based biomechanics lectures. For Lecture 1 and Lecture 2, students will watch multiple modules while navigating with positioning sensors and answer questions on a touch screen. Their eye-tracking data will be collected to analyze their attention during learning. For Lecture 3, students will learn advanced biomechanics calculations and participate in hands-on exercises.

Fall 2023 Experiments



Students watched multiple modules while navigating with positioning sensors and answered questions on a touch screen. Their eye-tracking and motion data were collected to analyze their learning patterns.

Fall 2022 Experiments



We recruited thirty students to participate in Lecture 1 and Lecture 2. They watched multiple modules while navigating with positioning sensors and answered questions on paper.
