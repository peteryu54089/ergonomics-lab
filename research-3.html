Integrating Eye-Tracking and Motion Capture Technologies

March 2023

We used a customized, lightweight eye-tracking device to enhance the accuracy of eye-tracking data collected from users engaged with the AR instructional modules. Utilizing a 3D printer, we fabricated specialized eyeglass frames and holders to integrate the eye-tracking device seamlessly with the AR headset, ensuring the device's camera was appropriately aligned with the headset's visual field. To facilitate the real-time acquisition of combined eye-tracking and motion-capture data, we crafted a communication application utilizing the TCP protocol in the C# programming language, interfacing with Ergoneers' D-Lab software. Leveraging this data, our objective is to explore and forecast users' learning behaviors and patterns, subsequently offering instantaneous feedback on their learning progress directly through the AR module.
